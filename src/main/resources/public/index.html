Instructions

To play battleship you first need to select where you are going to place your ships

Go to "Place Ships", select a ship, a row, column and orientation, the press "Place".

Make sure no ships overlap and no ships go out of bounds

To start the game, move to Fire

Select the row and column you think your opponents ships are placed in, then press "Fire"

If you hit your opponents ship the square will turn red, if you miss it will turn green

To win you must hit all your opponents ships
